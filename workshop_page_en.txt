[h1]Description[/h1]
[list]
[*]The Room System functions similarly to the one in *Oxygen Not Included*.
[*]Within a closed area constructed by walls of a specific size, you can form specific rooms by placing required items, buildings, etc.
[*]Different rooms offer different effects.
[/list]
